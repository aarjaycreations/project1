MY PARTY

The Nationalist Congress Party is one of the few political parties in the country to have made a significant presence in the Indian political arena in a short span of time. The NCP was formed on June 10, 1999 and had to face Lok Sabha and Assembly elections within two months. The party fielded candidates from different parts of the country. It was a huge success and gained nationwide recognition. At the center, in Maharashtra and some other states, the NCP became a part of the government. The party had the opportunity to represent the people of Goa, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Manipur, Assam, Gujarat, Kerala, Bihar, Orissa, Jharkhand and Arunachal. The NCP is the only party to get the approval of the Election Commission in such a short span of time. The NCP was conceived under the dynamic leadership of Shri Sharad Pawar Saheb, a veteran of Indian politics. Although the party went to the polls soon after its inception, it was due to the hard work and efficiency of its leaders that the party was able to win the mandate of the people of the country.

The brilliant performance of the Nationalist Congress Party and the mandate of the people were the major factors that compelled the Election Commission of India to grant Nationalist Party status to the Nationalist Congress Party. The popularity of the NCP is a result of the respect and affection that people have for Sharad Pawar. Whenever a natural calamity or catastrophe strikes the country or the state, Shri Pawar Saheb is the first to reach the affected areas and is ready to help the people and solve all the serious problems. The enactment of the National Food Security Act of 2013 shows Pawar's compassion and concern for the common man and the poor. The NCP has a leader who dreams of development and makes it a reality. The party and the people are the real beneficiaries. No other party has such a visionary leader who is experienced, studious, dynamic, balanced, strong, active and most importantly aware of the needs and aspirations of the people. He has the support of an effective team of good and capable administrators. That is why the NCP has been able to build trust among the people.
